Privacy Policy

Stride  ·  Effective April 30, 2026

The short version

Stride is built on a simple principle: your thoughts stay on your device. All recording, transcription, and AI analysis happens locally — nothing is sent to our servers or any third party. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

What we access and why

Microphone

Stride records your voice so you can capture thoughts while walking. Audio is processed entirely on-device for transcription. Recordings are stored locally in your app's private storage and are never uploaded.

Motion & Fitness

The pedometer detects walking to automatically start and stop recording sessions. Step counts are stored locally as part of your walk history. No location coordinates are collected or stored.

Location (approximate)

Stride requests location access solely to support motion detection on some device configurations. No GPS coordinates are recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Reminders

When you choose to add action items to Apple Reminders, Stride writes directly to your Reminders app on-device. No reminder data is transmitted externally.

Notifications

Stride sends local notifications for streak reminders and weekly recaps. These are scheduled on your device — no push notification servers are involved.

On-device AI

Transcription (speech-to-text) and walk analysis (summaries, action items, key points) are performed by AI models that run entirely on your device. Your voice data and journal content never leave your phone for AI processing.

Data storage

All app data — recordings, transcripts, walk summaries, and settings — is stored in your device's private local storage. This data is included in your standard iCloud or iTunes device backup if you have backups enabled. Stride does not operate its own cloud storage for user content.

Crash reporting

Stride may collect anonymous crash reports to help identify and fix bugs. These reports contain technical diagnostic information (device type, OS version, stack traces) and do not include your journal content, recordings, or any personally identifiable information.

Children

Stride is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of Stride after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
